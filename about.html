About the Initiative

Executive Leadership

Broad Shoulders Initiative is led by a small team of student leaders passionate about mental health advocacy, storytelling, and creating supportive communities where students feel comfortable sharing their experiences.

Jonathan Wang Founder & Executive Director Jonathan founded Broad Shoulders Initiative after his experiences with body image, injury recovery, athletic identity, and mental health changed how he understood student support. His story continues to shape the organization's commitment to honest storytelling and community.
