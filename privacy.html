Privacy & Submission Policy

Stories should be shared with care, consent, and respect.

Broad Shoulders Initiative exists to make student mental health feel less hidden. These guidelines explain how submitted stories are reviewed and protected before they are published.

Submission Expectations

  • Stories may be lightly edited for grammar, formatting, readability, privacy, or safety.
  • Anonymous submissions will remain anonymous unless the writer gives clear permission to be named.
  • Submissions may not be published immediately. Each story is reviewed before it appears on the site.
  • Harmful, hateful, unsafe, or disrespectful content will not be published.

Mental Health Disclaimer

Broad Shoulders Initiative is a storytelling and awareness platform. It is not a crisis service, medical provider, or substitute for professional mental health care.

If you or someone nearby may be in danger, please contact emergency services, a trusted adult, a school counselor, a licensed professional, or a crisis support resource.

Consent & Privacy

  • Please avoid sharing names, schools, teams, family details, or identifying information you are uncomfortable making public.
  • Submitting a story gives Broad Shoulders Initiative permission to review, lightly edit, and publish it.
  • Every submission is reviewed before publication with attention to consent, privacy, clarity, and emotional safety.

Respectful Community Standards

  • No harassment, hate speech, or language that targets someone for who they are.
  • No graphic harmful content or content that could encourage unsafe behavior.
  • Stories should be told with care for the writer, the people mentioned, and the students who may read them.
