Partnerships

Collaborating with organizations that share our commitment to mental health awareness, storytelling, student support, and reducing stigma.

Why Collaboration Matters

Trusted resources can make vulnerable stories feel less alone.

Broad Shoulders Initiative collaborates with organizations aligned with mental health awareness, student storytelling, athlete mental health, emotional wellbeing, and reducing stigma. These relationships help connect reflective stories with support networks that encourage honest conversation, peer care, and the courage to ask for help.

Resource Spotlight

Morgan's Message

Taking A Shot At Mental Health®

Morgan's Message amplifies stories, resources, and expertise to confront student-athlete mental health, build a community by and for athletes, and provide a platform for advocacy.

Its vision is to eliminate stigma around student-athlete mental health and help athletics treat mental health with the same urgency, care, and respect as physical health.

The overlap with Broad Shoulders Initiative is natural: athlete mental health, injury recovery, performance anxiety, identity beyond sports, peer support, vulnerability, and storytelling all sit near the center of both missions.

Jonathan Wang, founder of Broad Shoulders Initiative, is a Morgan's Message student-athlete ambassador and helps support the Morgan's Message chapter at Del Norte High School (DNHS). That connection keeps this spotlight grounded in local student leadership and everyday conversations, not just a link to another organization.

For a fuller overview of Morgan's Message programs and resources, the brochure is available as a supporting resource.

Programs

Ways Morgan's Message opens the conversation.

Education Program & Student-Athlete Ambassadors

Morgan's Message supports school and campus communities through education, chapter work, and student-athlete ambassadors who help create space for honest mental health conversations among peers.

The Mental Matchup

The Mental Matchup is Morgan's Message's podcast and blog, sharing athlete mental health stories so other athletes can hear lived experience, perspective, and reminders that they are not alone.

Why This Partnership Matters

Stories and support belong near each other.

Broad Shoulders Initiative features athlete-focused stories and reflections that speak honestly about injury, pressure, anxiety, identity, and recovery. Morgan's Message provides trusted mental health resources and student-athlete support for the moments when a story opens a deeper question.

Together, that means students can feel less alone, hear open discussion modeled with care, and find a path toward trusted support resources when they need more than reflection.
