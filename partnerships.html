Resource Spotlight

Morgan's Message Resource Spotlight

Taking A Shot At Mental Health®

Morgan's Message is a student-athlete mental health nonprofit centered on storytelling, education, ambassador leadership, and resources for athletes.

Broad Shoulders spotlights Morgan's Message because athlete pressure, injury recovery, identity beyond sport, peer support, and vulnerability are part of the stories shared here.

Jonathan Wang, founder of Broad Shoulders Initiative, is a Morgan's Message student-athlete ambassador supporting the Del Norte High School chapter. This spotlight reflects shared mission and student ambassador connection, not a formal legal partnership.

For an overview of Morgan's Message programs and resources, view the approved brochure.

View Morgan's Message Brochure

What They Center

  • Student-athlete mental health storytelling
  • Advocacy and stigma reduction in athletics
  • Community by and for athletes
  • Peer-to-peer conversation and support
  • Resources that help athletes ask for care

Programs

Ways Morgan's Message opens the conversation.

Education Program & Student-Athlete Ambassadors

Education, chapter work, and student-athlete ambassadors help school communities talk honestly about mental health.

The Mental Matchup

The podcast and blog share athlete mental health stories, perspective, and reminders that athletes are not alone.

Why This Resource Spotlight Matters

Stories and support belong near each other.

Broad Shoulders publishes athlete-focused reflections about injury, pressure, anxiety, identity, and recovery. Morgan's Message offers resources for students who want to keep learning or ask for help.

The goal is simple: help students feel less alone and make next steps easier to find.
