Initiatives

Focused storytelling, built with care before it begins.

Broad Shoulders initiatives will bring student voices together around shared themes while keeping consent, review, privacy, resources, and emotional safety close to every story.

Why Storytelling Campaigns Matter

A collection of honest voices can challenge silence.

A focused storytelling initiative can help students recognize shared experiences, open conversations, and find trusted support. Broad Shoulders is building the safety and editorial foundation first, before inviting a larger community to participate.

Safety comes before publishing.

Future initiatives will use the same core expectations: submissions are reviewed, publication is not guaranteed, contributors may request removal, and Broad Shoulders is not a crisis service. Visit the Privacy & Submission Policy and Resources before sharing something sensitive.
