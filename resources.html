Resources

Support should feel reachable, honest, and human.

Start here, then reach out to a trusted adult, counselor, coach, or qualified professional when more support is needed.

Immediate Safety

Broad Shoulders Initiative is not a crisis service or a substitute for professional care. If someone may be in immediate danger, call emergency services. In the United States, call or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline, or text HOME to 741741 for Crisis Text Line support.

Featured Student-Athlete Resource

Morgan's Message Resource Spotlight

Morgan's Message creates student-athlete mental health education, ambassador leadership, stories, and resources. Broad Shoulders highlights their work because athlete identity, pressure, injury, and support are part of the stories shared here.
