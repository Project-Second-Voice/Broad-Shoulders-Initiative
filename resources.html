Important Disclaimer

Broad Shoulders Initiative is not a therapy provider, medical organization, crisis service, or substitute for professional mental health care. If you or someone else may be in immediate danger, call emergency services right away. In the United States, the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline offers 24/7 call, text, and chat support. If you need ongoing support, please reach out to a trusted adult, school counselor, licensed professional, or local health provider.

Crisis and Immediate Help

For urgent emotional support in the United States, call or text 988, or use the Lifeline chat.
